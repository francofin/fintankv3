Fintank's Philosophy

FINTANK is created to provide cutting edge investment analysis techniques to the broad investment community. While our hope is that we can add value to your understanding of the market, we also seek to educate the investment community on quantitative topics in investment management, portfolio and market analysis.
At FINTANK we believe that investors should have a one stop access point for all their investing research tools. I created this website in order to provide you with the techniques and analysis tools that is used in the professional setting. We seek to provide you with tools such as:

  • Stock and Industry Research
  • Economic Indicators
  • Breaking Financial and Political News That can affect your Portfolio
  • Backtest and Build Your Own Portfolio To Keep Track of your Investment success
